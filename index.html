About

Symbiosis International University, Dubai

Symbiosis International (Deemed University) was established in 1971 by Prof. (Dr.) S.B. Mujumdar, for ‘Promoting International Understanding through Quality Education’. The Dubai campus is known for its excellent infrastructure and international exposure and is one of the Indian universities in UAE. With various national and international accolades to their name, Symbiosis International University, Dubai has set a standard for excellence in education and earned their name amongst top Indian universities in Dubai.

Centrally located in the heart of Dubai Knowledge Park, the campus carries forward a legacy of 50+ years of educational excellence to a dynamic city. The exact symbiosis international university address is Symbiosis International University, Building 14 Dubai Knowledge Park.

The campus offers various internationally accredited programmes in computer science, psychology, management, media studies, and engineering. Therefore, you are likely to find this university on every list of Indian universities approved by the UAE.

Admission process

Symbiosis International University Dubai
Admission Process

To get admitted into one of the Symbiosis International University courses you have to follow the admission process of the university. This process is mentioned in detail below to help you familiarise yourself with it.

Undergraduate

UG Admission Process

01

Submit Application

Upload required documents: Grade 12 results, Grade 10 results, English proficiency (80% in High School English or IELTS 5.0 / TOEFL 500), SOP, recommendation letter, and passport copy.

02

Application Review

The Admissions Team evaluates your application and supporting documents.

03

Receive Conditional Offer

Eligible applicants receive a Conditional Offer Letter and Scholarship Letter (if applicable, based on predicted or final school results).

04

Confirm Acceptance

Pay AED 250 (non-refundable) + 10% of annual tuition fees (before scholarship adjustment).

05

Provisional Admission

Admission status updated to Provisionally Admitted upon payment. Invoice and receipt issued by the Finance Office.

06

Final Admission Confirmation

Issued after payment of the first installment, visa fees (if applicable), caution deposit, and submission of original documents at orientation.

Postgraduate

PG Admission Process

01

Submit Application

Upload required documents: bachelor’s transcripts, English proficiency (IELTS 6.0 / TOEFL 550), SOP, recommendation letter, CV (if applicable), and passport copy.

02

Application Review & Interview

Applications are reviewed by the Admissions Team. Eligible candidates attend an academic interview.

03

Receive Conditional Offer

Selected applicants receive a Conditional Offer Letter and Scholarship Letter (if applicable).

04

Confirm Acceptance

Pay AED 250 (non-refundable) + 10% of annual tuition fees (before scholarship adjustment).

05

Provisional Admission

Admission confirmed upon payment. Invoice and receipt issued by Finance Office.

06

Final Admission Confirmation

Issued after payment of first installment, visa fees (if applicable), caution deposit, and submission of original documents.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are degrees from Symbiosis International University, Dubai globally recognised?
Yes. Degrees are awarded by Symbiosis International (Deemed University), a globally ranked institution recognised for academic excellence and international credibility.
What programmes are offered at Symbiosis International University, Dubai?
The university offers undergraduate programmes including BBA, BBA Dual Degree, BCA, BAMC, B.Com (with ACCA), B.Com (Hons) with ACCA, BSc Psychology (Hons), B.Tech in Computer Engineering, and a postgraduate MBA programme.
What is the admission process for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes?
The process includes application submission, document review, issuance of a conditional offer (if eligible), fee payment for confirmation, provisional admission, and final admission confirmation upon completion of required payments and document verification.
Is English proficiency mandatory for admission?
Yes. Applicants must meet the required English proficiency criteria (IELTS / TOEFL) or equivalent academic English requirements, depending on the programme level.
Does the university provide scholarship opportunities?
Yes, eligible students may receive a Symbiosis International University, Dubai scholarship based on academic performance, predicted grades, or previous qualifications.
